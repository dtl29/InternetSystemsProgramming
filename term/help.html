This web page is to make magic the gathering decks and share them with others on the site.


To view the decks the will display as tables with the card names within the bulk of them.


To create a deck go to My Decks and click on create. then while selecting cards by drag drop continue to click find card for the decklist to update
you can also hover over the mouse to ass or subtract a card, after doing this click on the find cards again to see the update. once the deck is complete with
the constrants of the magic the gatehring rules, then click on create to add that deck to your decklist and to the public decks.


you have to login to create decks, from the main page there is a signup button that will allow you to create an acount, if you already have one it will let
you login.
